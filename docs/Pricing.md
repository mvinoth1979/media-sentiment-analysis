## Pricing by Phase

### Phase 1 — News monitoring (complete ✅)

News portals, 6 languages, RBAC, state filtering, CSV export, email alerts, self-serve brand wizard — all live. Founder pricing window was open.

---

### Phase 2.0 — YouTube integration (complete ✅ — 2026-06-18)

YouTube video search, channel RSS, comment monitoring, YouTube-aware NLP, source type filter, YouTube reach metadata, YouTube KPI card, per-brand YouTube config in brand wizard — all live. **Included in all existing tiers at no additional charge.**

The pricing tiers are now renamed "News + YouTube" to reflect this. The founder pricing window remains technically open (no paying customers yet) but closes after first invoice.

| Tier | Founder Price | Standard Price |
|---|---|---|
| News + YouTube Essentials | ₹4,500/month | ₹6,500/month |
| News + YouTube Professional | ₹10,000/month | ₹14,000/month |
| Agency (5 brands, news + YouTube) | ₹32,000/month | ₹45,000/month |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

---

### Phase 2.1 — Reddit (planned, next)

Reddit's API is free and requires no approval. Adds a meaningful public-forum dimension (especially for tech and finance brands). No plan to increase pricing — Reddit will be included in all existing tiers as an additional source channel, just like YouTube.

---

### Phase 3 — Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook (planned, pricing increase)

When these ship, a **Full Social + News** tier is added. This directly competes with Locobuzz/Konnect's core product. Existing tier customers' founder-locked rates are preserved; new customers pay full rate.

| Tier | Price (post-Phase 3) |
|---|---|
| News + YouTube Professional | ₹16,000/month (existing customers locked at signup price) |
| Full Social + News Standard (1 brand, Twitter + Instagram + Facebook + YouTube + news) | ₹35,000/month |
| Full Social + News Pro (1 brand, all channels, all 6 languages) | ₹50,000/month |
| Agency Social + News (5 brands, all channels) | ₹1,40,000/month |
| Enterprise | Custom, ₹2,00,000+/month |

---

### Phase 4 — Full feature parity (export formats, PDF reports, API, competitive tracking)

At full parity with Locobuzz/Konnect mid-tier, standard pricing increases 20–30% across all tiers. Founder-locked customers retain their rate permanently.
