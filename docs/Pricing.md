## Pricing by Phase

### Phase 1 — Now (news monitoring, founder pricing active)

| Tier | Founder Price | Standard Price |
|---|---|---|
| News Essentials | ₹4,500/month | ₹6,500/month |
| News Professional | ₹10,000/month | ₹14,000/month |
| Agency (5 brands) | ₹32,000/month | ₹45,000/month |

Founder pricing is locked permanently for customers who sign up before social media launches. Use this as a conversion lever.

---

### Phase 2 — After social media (Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube) launches

Add a **Social + News** tier. Existing news-only tiers continue at their locked rates.

| Tier | Price |
|---|---|
| News Professional (existing) | Locked at signup price |
| Social + News Standard (1 brand, 3 social + all news) | ₹30,000/month |
| Social + News Pro (1 brand, all channels, all 6 languages) | ₹45,000/month |
| Agency Social + News (5 brands, all channels) | ₹1,20,000/month |
| Enterprise | Custom, ₹2,00,000+/month |

---

### Phase 3 — After full feature parity (export, alerts, API, competitive tracking)

At full parity with Locobuzz/Konnect mid-tier, standard pricing increases 20–30% across all tiers. Founder-locked customers retain their rate.
